Yu Shang 商宇

I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Electronic Engineering at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Yong Li and Prof. Jiansheng Chen. I also hold a secondary B.E. degree from the School of Economics and Management at Tsinghua University.

My current research focuses on world models and multimodal unified models, with the goal of building intelligent multimodal systems that can integrate perception, reasoning, and action.
