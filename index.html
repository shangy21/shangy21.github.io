Biography

I am a final-year Ph.D. student at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Yong Li and Prof. Jiansheng Chen. My research interests mainly focus on World Model, VLM, and LLM Agent.

Previously, I received my B.E. degree from the Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University in June 2021. I also received a second B.E. degree from the School of Economics and Management, Tsinghua University in June 2021.
