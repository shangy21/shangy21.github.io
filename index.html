Biography

I am a Ph.D. student at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Yong Li and Prof. Jiansheng Chen. I'm interested in multimodal large language models, LLM agents and recommender systems.

Previously, I received my B.E. degree from the Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University in June 2021.

I had research experiences on recommender systems, MLLMs, LLM reasoning, LLM agents and world models.
