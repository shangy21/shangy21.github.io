About News Publications Projects Service

Yu Shang 商宇

I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Electronic Engineering at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Yong Li and Prof. Jiansheng Chen. I also hold a secondary B.E. degree from the School of Economics and Management at Tsinghua University.

My research centers on multimodal intelligence, especially how machines perceive, simulate, and act in complex environments. My current interests include interactive world models, embodied AI, vision-language models, and multimodal agent systems.

I am broadly interested in building AI systems that connect world understanding, physical simulation, personalized interaction, and real-world decision making.
