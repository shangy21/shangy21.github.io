Yu Shang

商宇

I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Electronic Engineering at Tsinghua University, where I am advised by Prof. Yong Li and Prof. Jiansheng Chen. Simultaneously, I hold a secondary B.E. degree from the School of Economics and Management at Tsinghua University.

Driven by the vision of Multimodal Intelligence, my research bridges how machines perceive, simulate, and act. This trajectory unfolds from developing interactive world models to simulate physical realities, through leveraging VLMs for deep world understanding, to architecting multimodal agent systems for real-world interaction.
